The Walpole Robotics Foundation competes in the FIRST Robotics Competition throughout the winter months and shows off their robots around New England in their spare time. They spend 6 weeks designing, building, and programming their robot, then spend the next 7 weeks competing against other teams at the regional, district, and world championship level.
